Read Me

With great power comes great responsibility. Most internet marketers are shady characters that we must always avoid and never become. Avoid unethical marketing and business strategies such as

  • Review bombing
  • Manipulating google algorithms for short term gain
  • Dumping negative backlinks on competitors
  • Bulk content submission and bots for ourselves or against others
  • Keyword stuffing
  • Posting on shady websites

Even more serious are things to avoid are

  • Burying criminal records
  • Posting false information (libel, defamation of character, etc.)

This could happen to you. Be warned.

How to backlink

Most backlinks that we used had keywords within the a href element. However, one of our partners made the title of a blog post page the keyword with a link directly to that blog post.

  • Link with URL Text (Social media and directories only allows these type of back links)
    • https://www.dehumidifierreviews.com
    • https://dehumidifierreviews.com
    • www.dehumidifierreviews.com
    • dehumidifierreviews.com
    • Link with Page Title Text
      • Dehumidifier Reviews
      • Dehumidifier Review
    • Link with Targeted Keyword Text
      • Dehumidifier Reviews for Flooded Basements
      • Kids Bedroom Dehumidifier Reviews
